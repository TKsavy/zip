Anonymous Listening Test

Voice Conversion Evaluation

Instructions

Please complete the evaluation in a quiet environment using headphones or good speakers. Listen to the source, reference, and each anonymous system sample before assigning scores.

请在安静的环境中使用耳机或质量较好的扬声器完成评测。打分前，请先听源语音、参考语音以及每个匿名系统的转换语音。

  • Use a 5-point scale with 0.5 increments: 1.0, 1.5, 2.0, ..., 5.0. / 请使用 1.0 到 5.0 的五分制，步长为 0.5。
  • Each slider starts at 0, which means not scored. Move it to 1.0-5.0 to record a score. / 每个滑块初始为 0，表示尚未打分；请移动到 1.0-5.0 记录分数。
  • Evaluate each anonymous system independently. Do not score the source or reference. / 请独立评价每个匿名系统，不需要给源语音或参考语音打分。
  • System names are hidden on purpose. Please do not try to infer the real systems. / 系统真实名称已隐藏，请不要尝试推测真实系统。
  • You may replay samples, but avoid changing scores repeatedly after long comparison. / 可以重复播放，但请避免长时间反复比较后频繁修改分数。
  • Keep playback volume comfortable and consistent across samples. / 请保持舒适且一致的播放音量。
  • Your scores are saved automatically in this browser until you clear them. / 分数会自动保存在当前浏览器中，直到你清除保存的分数。

MOS / Naturalness

Judge audio quality and naturalness only.

  1. 5: Natural, clean, and human-like.
  2. 4: Mostly natural, with minor artifacts.
  3. 3: Understandable, but clearly synthetic or degraded.
  4. 2: Unnatural, noisy, or difficult to listen to.
  5. 1: Very poor quality or hard to understand.

只评价语音质量和自然度。

  1. 5：自然、清晰，接近真人语音。
  2. 4：整体自然，只有少量瑕疵。
  3. 3：能听懂，但明显合成或有失真。
  4. 2：不自然、有噪声，或听起来比较困难。
  5. 1：质量很差或难以理解。

Speaker Similarity

Compare with the reference speaker. Judge voice identity and speaking style; ignore background noise or recording condition.

  1. 5: Same or very similar speaker.
  2. 4: Mostly similar speaker, with small differences.
  3. 3: Partly similar, but noticeable differences.
  4. 2: Mostly different speaker.
  5. 1: Completely different speaker.

与参考语音中的说话人比较。评价声音身份和说话风格，忽略背景噪声或录音条件。

  1. 5：同一个或非常相似的说话人。
  2. 4：大体相似，但有小差异。
  3. 3：部分相似，但差异明显。
  4. 2：大部分听起来像不同说话人。
  5. 1：完全不同的说话人。

Accent Similarity

For L2Arctic only. Compare accent with the reference, including pronunciation, rhythm, and stress.

  1. 5: Accent is very similar to the reference.
  2. 4: Accent is mostly similar.
  3. 3: Accent is partly similar, with clear differences.
  4. 2: Accent is mostly different.
  5. 1: Accent is completely different.

仅用于 L2Arctic。与参考语音比较口音，包括发音、节奏和重音。

  1. 5：口音与参考语音非常相似。
  2. 4：口音大体相似。
  3. 3：口音部分相似，但差异明显。
  4. 2：口音大部分不同。
  5. 1：口音完全不同。

仅用于 ESD。与参考语音比较情感，包括情感类别和强度。

  1. 5：情感与参考语音非常相似。
  2. 4：情感大体相似。
  3. 3：情感部分相似，但差异明显。
  4. 2：情感大部分不同。
